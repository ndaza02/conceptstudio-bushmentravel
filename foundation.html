About Us

Turning Tourism into Impact

The Bushmen Foundation is the charitable arm of Bushmen Travel Company, founded in 2024 to support communities and conserve the unique landscapes of the Matobo region. Based in Bulawayo, we turn sustainable tourism into measurable social and environmental impact.

What We Do

Program Highlights

Education Support

Partner with local schools to provide learning materials, classroom renovations, teacher support, and cultural exchange programs with responsible visitors.

Conservation & Anti-Poaching

Support habitat restoration, wildlife monitoring, and community-focused anti-poaching awareness campaigns developed with local conservation partners.

Infrastructure & Basic Needs

Install boreholes, solar systems, and community spaces that improve health, education, and sustainable livelihoods.

Income-Generating Projects

Develop eco-tourism initiatives, craft cooperatives, and small-scale agricultural projects to create sustainable jobs and local ownership.
